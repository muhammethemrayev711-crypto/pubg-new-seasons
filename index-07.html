NARUTO

SEASON SCENARIO: NINJA CLASH (FLAME AND SAND)[cite: 10]

1. INTRODUCTION AND ATMOSPHERE

The Erangel map is split in two by a mysterious natural phenomenon. Half of the map is engulfed in a golden-orange dawn, where the Village of Flame rises, while the other half descends into a crimson dusk, dominated by swirling sandstorms and the emergence of the Village of Sand[cite: 10].

Lobby Screen: A massive pillar of energy pierces the sky at the center. On the right, Naruto's Kurama (Nine-Tailed) form, and on the left, Gaara's Shukaku form roar at each other incessantly. Players stand in the neutral zone between these two titans, wearing the gear of their chosen village[cite: 10].

2. SELECTION AND SQUAD STRUCTURE

  • Village Loyalty: Players choose either the Village of Flame or the Village of Sand at the start of the season. Squads can only be formed by members of the same village[cite: 10].
  • The Choice of Fate: As the match begins, the system randomly selects one player from each squad to be the "Jinchūriki" (Vessel). A silhouette of either Naruto or Gaara appears over this player[cite: 10].

3. GAMEPLAY MECHANICS

Transformation: Once the "Jinchūriki" reaches a "Chakra Seal" location on the map, they transform into their massive beast form[cite: 10].

Special Abilities:

  • Flame (Naruto): Uses Shadow Clones to confuse enemies and Rasengan to destroy vehicles in a single strike[cite: 10].
  • Sand (Gaara): Uses Sand Shield to absorb incoming bullets and the Sand Eye to see enemies inside buildings (X-Ray effect)[cite: 10].

Collision Effect: When a Rasengan and a Sand Wall collide, a massive "Energy Explosion" occurs in a wide radius; both sides have their armor shattered and HP reduced. This adds a layer of tactical risk management[cite: 10].

4. LOOT AND ECONOMY (CHAKRA SYSTEM)

Chakra Crystals: The leaders (Naruto or Gaara) of the winning squad collect "Yellow Chakra" (Flame) or "Red Chakra" (Sand) as loot[cite: 10].

Deadly Cargo: These crystals are visible on the player's backpack. If the carrier dies, the crystal drops to the ground, allowing the enemy to steal it. This turns the final circle into a high-stakes "capture the flag" experience[cite: 10].

Reward Market: Collected crystals are used in the "End-of-Season Chakra Market"[cite: 10]:

  • Yellow Chakra Shop: Rasengan-themed weapon skins and special capes[cite: 10].
  • Red Chakra Shop: Sand Shield-themed helmet/backpack skins and sandstorm movement trails[cite: 10].

5. END-OF-SEASON FINALE: "THE GREAT CLASH"

In the final week of the season, a "Battleground" opens at the very center of the map[cite: 10].
All players worldwide contribute to their village's total crystal count to determine the winner[cite: 10].
Final Scene: At the end of the season, the lobby screen shows the winning beast (either Naruto or Gaara) overpowering the defeated side, showering the sky with a beacon of victory[cite: 10].

6. PLAYER PSYCHOLOGY AND BALANCE

  • Why the Choice is Difficult: Because the rewards for Yellow Chakra and Red Chakra are completely different. If a player wants both the Naruto skins and the Gaara effects, they must master both sides during the season[cite: 10].
  • Social Interaction: The massive lobby animation encourages pre-match strategy talk, such as "Where should we deploy our clones?" or "When should we activate the wall?"[cite: 10]
This scenario was submitted to PUBG Mobile on May 26th.[cite: 9]

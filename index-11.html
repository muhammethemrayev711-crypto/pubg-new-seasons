LABYRINTH - THE CHOICE OF DESTINY

SCENARIO: THE LABYRINTH - CHOICE OF FATE (FINAL VERSION)[cite: 16]

1. OPENING: SURVIVAL INSIDE THE LABYRINTH

At the start of the match, all paths to the central labyrinth are open. However, the "Chest Room" in the very center is locked for 5 minutes. During this time, players must battle each other while navigating moving walls and traps (pendulums, spears) that shift every minute[cite: 16].

2. THE CRITICAL 5-MINUTE MARK

When the 5 minutes expire, the center gate opens. The player who reaches the chest acquires legendary weapons (AWM, AMR, Groza, etc.) and the "Labyrinth Map." This map allows the player to predict the next movement of the walls (which paths will open or close)[cite: 16].

3. CHOICE OF FATE: NORMAL EXIT OR CURSED PORTAL?

Upon obtaining the map, the player faces a decision:

  • A) "Play it Safe": Use the map to predict wall movements, head to one of the 7 normal exits, and leave the labyrinth quietly[cite: 16].
  • B) "Cursed Portal": If the player feels overwhelmed by the traps, they can enter the Cursed Portal next to the chest[cite: 16].

4. THE PRICE OF THE CURSE

A player who uses the Cursed Portal is instantly teleported to a random location on the map. However, this "easy exit" comes with a cost: the player becomes Cursed[cite: 16].
Effect of the Curse: While looting or moving across Erangel, the ground may suddenly split, walls may rise, or spears may strike. The curse hunts the player until they are either eliminated or secure the "Winner Winner Chicken Dinner."[cite: 16]

5. THE CLEANSE DECISION (OPTIONAL)

If the map holder chooses to stay, saying, "I won't leave through the portal, I will conquer the entire labyrinth," sirens blare, and all traps in the labyrinth lock onto them. This is the ultimate "Choice of Fate": either survive and clear the gauntlet or be destroyed within the maze[cite: 16].
